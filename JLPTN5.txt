Grammar Lesson                  Meaning of the Grammar  

1. cha ikenai / ja ikenai       -   must not do
ちゃいけない・じゃいけない 

2. da / desu                    -   to (am; is; are; were used to)
だ・です 

3. dake                         -   only; just; as much as
だけ 

4. darou                        -   I think; it seams; probably; right?
だろう 

5. de                           -   in; at; on; by; with; via ~
で

6. demo                          -  but; however; though ~    
でも

7. deshou                       -   I think; it seams; probably; right?
でしょう

8. donna                        -   what kind; what sort
どんな

9. doushite                     -   why; for what reason; how
どうして

10. douyatte                    -   how; in what way; by what means
どうやって
================================================================
11. ga                          -   subject marker; however; but
が

12. ga arimasu                  -   there is; non-living things
があります

13. ga hoshii                   -   to what something
がほしい

14. ga imasu                    -   there is; living things
がいます

15. hou gaii                    -   had better; it'd be better to
ほうがいい

16. ichiban                     -   the most; the best
一番・いちばん

17. isshoni                     -   together
一緒に・いっしょに

18. itsumo                      -   always; habitually
いつも

19. janai / dewa nai            -   to not be (am; not; is not)
じゃない・でわない
================================================================
20. ka                          -   question particle
か

21. ka ~ ka                     -   or
か～か

22. kara                        -   because; since; from
から

23. kata                        -   the way of doing something; how to do
方々・かた

24. kedo                        -   but; however; although
けど

25. keredo mo                   -   but; however; although
けれども

26. mada                        -   still; not yet
まだ

27. mada ~te imasen             -   have not yet ~
まだ～ていません

28. made                        -   until; as far as; to (an extent); even; ~
まで


29. mae ni                      -   before; in front of
前に・まえに

30. masen ka                    -   would you; do you want to
ませんか

31. mashou                      -   let's; shall we ~
ましょう

32. mashou ka                   -   shal l ~;
ましょうか

33. mo                          -   too; also; as well as 
も

34. mou                         -   already; anymore; again;
もう

35. naa                         -   sentence ending particle
なあ

36. naide                       -   without doing
ないで

37. naide kudasai               -   please don't do
ないでください

38. naito ikenai                -   must do; have obligation to do
ないといけない

39. naku temo ii                -   don't have to
なくてもいい

40. naku cha                    -   must do; need to; gotta do
なくちゃ

41. naku te wa ikenai           -   must do; need to do
なくてはいけない

42. naku te wa naranai          -   must do; need to do
なくてはならない

43. naru                        -   to become
なる

44. ndesu                       -   to explain something
んです

45. ne                          -   isn't it?; right?; eh?
ね

46. ni                          -   destination particle; in; at; on; to
に

47. ni iku                      -   go to do
に行く・にいく

48. ni suru                     -   to decide
にする

49. ni / he                     -   to (indicates direction / destanation)
に・へ

50. no                          -   possisive particle
の

51. no desu                     -   to explain something; shall emphasis
のです
